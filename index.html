Planerist

The planerist is a man who tries to survive as much as possible, fighting over his life in the wild sky with raged red birds. He has three lives at the beginning of the game. One level lasts one minute. At each next level count of birds and its speed becomming more and amore. You may try what level you will able to achive right now! Good look!

PLAY GAME

Controls

or A — move an airplane to the left side

or D — move an airplane to the right side

or W — move an airplane to the up side

or S — move an airplane to the down side

SPACE — open fire

Battery save

If you will leave current tab in your web browser during the game, it will be stopped automatically, so you won't warry about your battery life and will be able to continue your game by pressing play button when you will return.

Infinite levels

Play the game as many as you need. If you will improve your gaming skill, don't think that you will be able to finish the game. In the game was implemented infinite level system with constantly increasing difficult. So don't warry that the game will become not interesting for you. if you'll improve your skill, you always may try to get new level and earn more scores.
